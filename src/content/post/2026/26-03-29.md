Recently, I moved to a new city, and while I am still waiting for my furniture to arrive, including my PC, I had to find a way to appease my gaming obsession.


But the problem is, I just brought my old laptop that only has 8gb RAM and a Iris Xe. I also did not bring my mouse.

With this limitation, I had to be creative.
I decided to look for games with low-end requirements that can be played with a touchpad.
Then, realized two genres that could be handy: digital card games and tower defense.

I have a long history of card games that I could talk about, but this is a subject for another post.

Back to my game selection...
Well, I decided to play some tower defense games and went online to check some lists like "best tower defense games of all time", etc.
Then, I realized that I had already played most of them.

So, today I will show my top 5 tower defense games in no particular order of preference:

# 1. Kingdom Rush series
[![Watch the video](https://img.youtube.com/vi/tZbmGcU7KHs/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tZbmGcU7KHs)


Why I was obsessed:
* Gorgeous graphics
* Level diversity
* Multiple heroes to choose from

# 2. Orcs must die series
[![Watch the video](https://img.youtube.com/vi/cawZsLAAIbY/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cawZsLAAIbY)

Why I was obsessed:
* Good sense of humor
* My favorite traps of all tower defense games
* Fantastic character animations.

# 3. Dungeon Defenders 1
[![Watch the video](https://img.youtube.com/vi/dQBZKZ1-E7w/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQBZKZ1-E7w)

Why I was obsessed:
* You can place the traps almost anywhere
* Infinite loot
* Each hero has a different playstyle

# 4. Gemcraft series
[![Watch the video](https://img.youtube.com/vi/uwefGmHHnOk/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uwefGmHHnOk)

Why I was obsessed:
* Each game of the series has a myriad of levels
* You can fuse the effects of your towers
* It can get very difficult

# 5. Bloons TD 6
[![Watch the video](https://img.youtube.com/vi/4-cT9n8QNAk/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4-cT9n8QNAk)

Why I was obsessed:
* Charismatic monkeys
* Super relaxing to play
* High replayability
